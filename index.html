Shadows of Darkness

August 27, 2024, by Elena Lopez

In Our Share of Night, Mariana Enriquez immerses us in a dark and fascinating universe where horror and reality intertwine in a disturbing way. The novel, which blends elements of gothic fiction with Argentine history, follows a father and his son as they attempt to escape a secret society that seeks to control their mystical powers. The work stands out for Enriquez's ability to explore deep themes such as pain, loss, and familial inheritance. Through its pages, we confront the darkness that resides both in the supernatural world and in the hearts of men, creating an unsettling atmosphere that lingers with the reader long after the book is closed.
